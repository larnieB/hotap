About Us

Our Story

Founded in Thika, Hotap Collections was born from a passion for refined elegance and bold expression. Every collection we curate celebrates timeless craftsmanship, individuality, and the beauty of simplicity. From luxury dresses to statement accessories, Hotap stands for confidence, authenticity, and modern Kenyan sophistication.

Our Philosophy

We believe fashion is not just what you wear—it’s how you carry your story. Each piece is designed or selected to align with the Hotap vision: luxury that is inclusive, rooted in culture, and unapologetically bold. Our design language bridges classic silhouettes with modern textures, creating a harmony of comfort and confidence.

Our Mission

To redefine African luxury by merging global aesthetics with local craftsmanship, empowering our customers to express who they are through timeless fashion.
